## FAQs

### General Questions

**Q: Is AfriVerse free to use?**
A: Yes! Reading articles and accessing the AfriPulse Index is completely free. Some premium features may be introduced in the future.

**Q: How often is the AfriPulse Index updated?**
A: The index updates in real-time, with sentiment scores recalculated every hour based on the latest data.

**Q: Can I trust the Impact Scores?**
A: Yes. Community-reported impacts are verified by our editorial team before being counted. We also use multiple data sources to validate engagement and citation metrics.

### Impact Score Questions

**Q: Who can submit impact reports?**
A: Anyone can submit an impact report. You don't need an account, though having one helps us follow up if needed.

**Q: How long does verification take?**
A: Typically 24-72 hours. Complex impacts requiring external verification may take longer.

**Q: What happens if my impact report is rejected?**
A: You'll receive feedback explaining why. Common reasons include insufficient evidence or the impact not being directly attributable to the article.

### Storyteller Questions

**Q: I'm not a professional journalist. Can I still apply?**
A: Yes! We welcome bloggers, researchers, academics, and passionate writers. What matters is the quality of your work and your commitment to authentic African storytelling.

**Q: My application was rejected. Can I reapply?**
A: Yes, after 90 days. Use the feedback provided to strengthen your portfolio and application.

**Q: How do I contact my badge manager?**
A: Verified storytellers can reach out through the Writer Dashboard or email writer-support@afriverse.africa.

### Technical Questions

**Q: What browsers are supported?**
A: AfriVerse works best on Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge (latest versions). Mobile browsers are fully supported.

**Q: How do I report a bug?**
A: Use the feedback button in the footer or email bugs@afriverse.africa with details and screenshots.

**Q: Is there a mobile app?**
A: Coming soon! For now, our website is fully responsive and works great on mobile devices.
