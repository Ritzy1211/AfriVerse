As the **2025 Africa Cup of Nations** approaches, Super Eagles head coach **Finidi George** has unveiled his tactical blueprint, signaling a departure from the defensive setups of previous tournaments.

## The New Philosophy

In an exclusive interview with AfriVerse Sports, Finidi outlined his vision:

> "We have the talent to attack. Victor Osimhen, Ademola Lookman, Samuel Chukwuezeâ€”these players belong in an offensive system. I want us to dictate games, not react to them."

### Formation: 4-3-3

Finidi's preferred setup emphasizes:

- **High pressing** from the front three
- **Quick transitions** through midfield
- **Overlapping fullbacks** creating width
- **Goalkeeper involvement** in build-up play
