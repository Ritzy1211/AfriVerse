Nigerian music icon **David Adeleke**, popularly known as **Davido**, has officially announced his much-anticipated "Timeless" World Tour for 2025, promising fans an unforgettable experience across four continents.

## Tour Highlights

The announcement comes fresh off the massive success of his "Timeless" album, which has dominated charts worldwide. Here's what we know so far:

### Confirmed Cities

1. **Lagos, Nigeria** - Eko Atlantic (January 15, 2025)
2. **Accra, Ghana** - Independence Square (January 22, 2025)
3. **London, UK** - O2 Arena (February 14-15, 2025)
4. **Paris, France** - Accor Arena (February 20, 2025)
5. **New York, USA** - Madison Square Garden (March 8, 2025)
6. **Los Angeles, USA** - SoFi Stadium (March 15, 2025)
7. **Dubai, UAE** - Coca-Cola Arena (April 5, 2025)

## Ticket Information

Tickets go on sale **January 2, 2025** at 10 AM local time. VIP packages include:

- **Diamond Package** ($500): Front row seats, meet & greet, exclusive merchandise
- **Gold Package** ($250): Premium seating, signed poster
- **General Admission** ($75-150): Standard seating

> "This tour is for my fans who have supported me through everything. I'm bringing the full energy, the full production, everything!" - Davido

## Special Guests

While the full lineup hasn't been revealed, sources close to the production team suggest appearances from:

- Wizkid (select dates)
- Burna Boy
- Tems
- Rema

## Production Details

The tour will feature:

- State-of-the-art LED screens
- Custom stage design by Es Devlin
- Live band featuring 12 musicians
- Pyrotechnics and special effects
